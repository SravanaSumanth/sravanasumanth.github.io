Impedance Control Under Uncertainty about Environment Properties.

This is a thesis proposed as a part of my masters degree in Mechanical Engineering. This thesis proposes a method to successfully perform impedance control without changing the stiffness of the manipulator for every change in the environment. This thesis uses a moving desired configuration/frame to perform the tasks while keeping the stiffness of the manipulator as constant. Also, Screw Linear Interpolation (ScLERP) is used in choosing the desired configuration to ensure the required end effector trajectory.
